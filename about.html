About Me:

Hello there, my name is Laqwanda Nettles. I’m glad you want to know more about me. In all honesty, I’m just a very random person, and this website will definitely display that. I attended Nicholls State University for Culinary Arts. My first love is food! Yes, I'm a foodie, so look forward to seeing many recipes. I started baking at the age of 7 with my little Easy Bake Oven with my dad; it was our favorite activity to do. At age 10, I began cooking on my own and coming up with random recipes. One of my greatest hobbies, however, is learning and discovering new things. My new path has led me to software development. I am currently enrolled in a CodeX Academy boot camp. So, I hope you enjoy my random thoughts of : education, food, inspiration, and laughter.
